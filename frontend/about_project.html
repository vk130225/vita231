ML-Powered Parametric Insurance

Zero-Touch Protection for India's Gig Workers

VITA-INSURATECH is a mobile-first, parametric micro-insurance platform built exclusively for Q-Commerce delivery workers. We detect disruptions, validate claims across six independent layers, and process instant payouts — without the worker doing anything at all.

The Problem Nobody Is Solving

Every morning, thousands of Zepto and Blinkit delivery workers check the sky before their phones. If it's raining hard, they don't work. And if they don't work, they don't eat.

Existing insurance fails this demographic structurally:

  • Manual claim filing — workers mid-disruption don't open forms
  • Monthly pricing — gig workers earn weekly
  • No coverage for lost working hours from external events
